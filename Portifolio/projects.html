Online Retail Sales Performance Analysis Report

Business Problem

The online business has experienced a decline on how they operate on their daily business due to the Corona Virus. This caused a shift in the economy which led the business to cut back on employee numbers from 25 to 5 employees.

Analysis

This analysis will help us understand on how the sales number will be affected with fewer employees working in the company.
To evaluate the performance of the company I have prepared a dashboard with 5 KPI cards and with 4 visual charts and 2 slicers which will help in analysing data in months and years.

Insights

The performance of the sales is seasonal. From Janauary to February the business shows small sales performance and slowly increase from March and mid year round. By November the performance gradually increases and reaches the highest peak in December. On the right of the dashboard you wil notice that december ha sthe highest gross sales.

Recommendation:

The business should improve the quality of their products , this will help in reducing the amount stock returned and fewer losses improve the business and increase revenues. Also the business should get more stock than they have when approach December to use those busy months for more profit and benefit.

Online Retail Sales Performance Analysis Report

Business Problem

The online retails store wants to understand which product makes the most sales and peforms good even when there are discounts and returns.

Analysis

I analyzed the data by creating 4KPI cards that sumed up the net quantity, net sales ,gross sales , total discounts and total returns. To visualize the performance , I created 4 charts for analysis , patterns and decision making. This analysis is important and it will help the business to understand which product they should invest in , inorder for them to make profit. It will also help the store to fix their pricing , quality strategies and discounts.

Insights

From the analysis , Baskets generate most sales and net quantity while on the other side they have the most returns and discounts. This prove that the Basket are in high demand while on the other side they affect the profit of the business.

Recommendation:

The business should stock up more on Arts & Sculpture since they they have the strongest sales and lower discounts and returns. Since the Basket has the highest sales and highest returns and discounts , the business should investigate the root cause to see if it can be fixed to improve the discount and amount returned. Lastly the business should do promotion on products that have the lowest sales to improve the profit of the business.
